Meet the Visionaries

Two hearts, one mission - to restore dignity and prosperity to Indian agriculture through technology that truly understands the farmer's soul.

Satwika Chowdary

Founder & CEO

Agricultural Sciences • Rural Development

The Farmer's Daughter

"నా తాత గారు చెప్పేవారు - 'మట్టిని గౌరవించు, అది నిన్ను గౌరవిస్తుంది.' ఈ మాట నా హృదయంలో నేటికీ మారుమ్రోగుతుంది."

Growing up in a farming family in Andhra Pradesh, Satwika witnessed both the beauty and struggles of agricultural life. Her grandfather's wisdom about respecting the soil became the foundation of her life's mission.

Educational Journey

M.Sc. Agricultural Sciences, ANGRAU • Rural Development Certification, NIRD

Field Experience

5+ years working with farming communities across South India

Personal Mission

Bridging traditional farming wisdom with modern sustainable practices

First Field Visit Together • April 2022

Our Journey Timeline

From a chance meeting to transforming thousands of lives - every milestone tells a story of perseverance and purpose.

The Spark

March 2022

First meeting at Agricultural Conference in Delhi. Shared vision of farmer-centric AI technology.

Deep Dive Research

April - August 2022

6 months of intensive field research across 5 states, interviewing 500+ farmers to understand real challenges.

First Prototype

September 2022

Built MVP with basic soil analysis and crop recommendation features. First 50 farmers onboarded.

Rapid Growth

2023

Reached 5,000+ farmers across 10 states. Added multilingual voice assistant and weather integration.

Industry Recognition

2024

Won 'Best AgriTech Innovation' award. Featured in national media. Partnerships with agricultural universities.

Today & Beyond

2025

10,000+ farmers served. 40% average yield increase. Expanding to 15 states with advanced AI features.
