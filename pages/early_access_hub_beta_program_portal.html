Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our beta program and early access.

Is the beta program free for farmers? • क्या बीटा प्रोग्राम मुफ्त है?

Yes, completely free! We provide free access to all AI features, WhatsApp support, and training materials. No hidden costs or subscription fees during beta.

Do I need a smartphone to use Krishi Sevak?

A basic smartphone with WhatsApp is recommended, but we also provide voice call support and SMS updates for farmers with feature phones.

What languages are supported? • कौन सी भाषाएं समर्थित हैं?

We support Hindi, English, Telugu, Tamil, Kannada, Gujarati, Marathi, and Punjabi with voice recognition and text responses.

How quickly will I see results?

Most farmers see improvements in crop health within 2-4 weeks of following AI recommendations. Yield improvements are typically visible in the next harvest cycle.

What market data is available to investors?

Comprehensive market analysis including user growth metrics, farmer retention rates, yield improvement data, geographic expansion, and revenue projections.

How can I schedule a meeting with founders?

After submitting your investor application, our team will review your profile and schedule a meeting within 5-7 business days based on mutual availability.

What is the current valuation and funding status?

Detailed financial information and funding status are shared with qualified investors after initial screening and NDA execution.

What types of partnerships do you offer?

We offer technology integration partnerships, farmer outreach collaborations, research partnerships, and co-marketing opportunities with NGOs, government agencies, and corporates.

How does the pilot program work?

We start with a 3-month pilot program covering 100-500 farmers in your network, providing full platform access, training, and impact measurement tools.

What support do you provide to partners?

Dedicated partnership manager, technical integration support, training materials, marketing collateral, and regular impact reporting.
